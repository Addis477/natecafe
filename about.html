About Us

Discover the passion behind Nate Café & Restaurant

From Passion to Plate

Nate Café & Restaurant was born from a simple idea: bring together the finest Ethiopian ingredients with international culinary techniques. What started as a small coffee shop has grown into Addis Ababa's premier dining destination.

Today, we're proud to serve our community with authentic flavors, warm hospitality, and unforgettable experiences. Every dish tells a story of tradition, innovation, and love for great food.
